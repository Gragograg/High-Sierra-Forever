High Sierra Forever

About 6 months ago i saw this project Mavericks Forever And Well It looked very interesting tho I had a 2015 MacBook Air And on it i had macOS High Sierra and i thought why not make a similar thing but just with macOS High Sierra. And Well Here We Are

Why High Sierra?

When i got my MacBook Around 2019 it was clear to me that i should use the whole thing as much time as i could well that time ended in 2021 really when macOS Montery released when i realised that was the last version of macOS this MacBook would ever get Somewere around 2023 i found the Open Core Legacy Patcher (OCPL for short) i used it for around a year but i realised that it started to get very slow and it was unstable and as my needs started to rise i couldn't rely on this mac for very long so i decided to start downgrading it i started with macOS El Capitan, macOS Sierra and High Sierra. El Capitan and Sierra Really couldn't meet my needs but High Sierra was just perfect so High Sierra it is

Initial Setup

When you finish installing the macOS first thing to do is open terminal and run:

curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Gragograg/High-Sierra-Forever/refs/heads/main/postinstall.sh | sh

This script will

  • Update macOS to the latest version.
  • Change some default settings.
  • Remove stuff that don't work anymore.
